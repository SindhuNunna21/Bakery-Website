About Us

A Bit Of Heaven

Baked fresh in small batches throughout the day!

We care deeply about baking delicious cupcakes, creating sustainable community and joyous experiences.

These incredible cupcakes are light, fluffy, creamy, crumbly, and stay perfectly moist for 4 whole days which is unheard of for cupcakes!

Available for store pickup and local delivery!

When you send a cake, we know you aren’t just sending dessert. You are sending a moment of celebration, joy, healing and togetherness. When you can’t be there to celebrate in person, We Take The Cake makes it easy to delight your family, friends and colleagues with our freshly baked cakes.
